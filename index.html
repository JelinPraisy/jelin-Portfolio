The Smart Water and Juice vending Machine using FPGA

"The Smart Juice and Water Vending Machine Using FPGA is an efficient, cost-effective solution designed to provide customizable beverage dispensing while minimizing waste. Utilizing FPGA technology, the system integrates a 16x2 LCD, 4x4 keypad, motors, and relays to enable precise control over dispensing eight different water quantities (100ml - 2L) and four juice options (1-4 cups). Unlike conventional vending machines, it offers manual payment validation, ensuring affordability and ease of use in diverse settings like schools, railway stations, and malls."
